Interactive Learning Modules

Explore concepts with hands-on simulations. Adjust parameters and see how systems respond in real time.

These interactive modules help reinforce what we work on in tutoring sessions. Click a topic below to experiment with real-world scientific phenomena.

Predator–Prey Dynamics

Explore how prey and predator populations change together. Adjust parameters to see oscillations.

💡 Key concept

Prey grow when predators are few; predators grow when prey are abundant. This creates cycles.

Reaction Rate & Activation Energy

Increase temperature or catalyst strength to lower activation energy and speed up reactions.

💡 Key concept

Reactions cross an energy barrier (Ea). Higher temperature and catalysts both lower this barrier, speeding up the reaction.

Projectile Motion

Launch a projectile and see its trajectory. Adjust speed and angle to explore the physics.

💡 Key concept

With no air resistance, range is maximized near 45°. Horizontal and vertical motions combine to form the arc.
