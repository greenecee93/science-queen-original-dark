About Ms. Greene 👩🏽‍🏫✨

Welcome to the kingdom of science! I’m Crystal Greene, also known as Ms. Greene the Science Queen — a Charlotte‑based science educator and tutor specializing in Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Earth Science, AP Sciences, and Intro College STEM.

My mission is simple: make science clear, engaging, and confidence‑building for every student. Whether you're preparing for an exam, catching up, or aiming for mastery, I bring interactive tools, real‑world examples, and personalized strategies to help you succeed.
